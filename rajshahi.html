R ajshahi Division District List And Upazila List 2022. Rajshahi Division is an administrative region in northwestern Bangladesh. It has a population of about 20 million and an area of ​​16,154 sq km. Rajshahi Division consists of eight districts, six Upazilas, 59 municipalities, and 574 unions. Rajshahi, Bogra, Pabna, and Sirajganj are the four leading trading centers and major cities of the Rajshahi Division. Nature, Naogaon, Chapainawabganj, Joypurhat are the leading agricultural areas.
